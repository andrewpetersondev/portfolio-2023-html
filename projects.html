Measuring Markup and the Returns to Scale in the U.S. Storage Battery Manufacturing Industry 1958-2016

This paper shows the evolution of the markup of price over marginal cost and output elasticity of cost for the US storage battery manufacturing industry, which is NAICS sector 335911. Based on our research, we have found that firms in the storage battery manufacturing industry do not have significant market power, which can help explain why the markup in this sector decreases over the time period.
